Our team has been tasked with developing a robotic arm at an affordable price to be used in the Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University Robotics Lab as an educational tool and a demonstration on how to integrate AI into a robotic system. To achieve the goal of preparing students for industry, the arm must be programmed in Python, have object handling, and incorporate AI features. The Articulated Removable Manipulator (ARM) needs to be able to operate on a robotic base being developed by Octagon Differential Drive (ODD) Robot and on its own.
